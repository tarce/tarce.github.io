Overview

The Computer & Information Science & Engineering (CISE) Department offers a number of degree programs in which you will study computers and computational systems. Our main focus is on software; including their theory, design, development, and application. With a degree from the CISE Department you will be able to use your programming and algorithmic knowledge to tackle problems ranging from abstract to tangible in both academia and industry.
