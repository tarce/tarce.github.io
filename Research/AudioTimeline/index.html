3D Audio Timeline | Study Design

A look at study design in 3D audio over the years.

This timeline highlights some of the important research conducted in 3D Audio, with a specific focus on psychoacoustic localization studies. The focus of this timeline is to develop an understanding of important study design decisions and their impact on findings.

2007

April

  • Lord Rayleigh publishes the first sound localization study: On Our Perception of the Direction of a Source of Sound
