Variable Description Value(s)
Azimuth Horizontal angular distance from median plane. 0° to 360° @ 15° increments
Elevation Vertical angular distacne from horizontal plane. 54°, 36°, 18°, 0°,- 18°, and -36°
Distance The distance from the central point in the head. 1.38m radius
Sound Type The sound type used. 250ms Gaussian noise (20ms on/off ramp). 300ms silence between.
Sound Frequency The sound frequency used. Bandpassed with a tenth-order filter between 200Hz and 14kHz.

Results

Reference paper for full results. The basic premise is that HRTFs gives similar localizations to free-field. Given other studies, the results should be viewed as inconclusive. Middlebrooks provides and overview of various similar studies.

Design Decisions

In the context of other similar studies, due to differences in methodology, results should be viewed with scepticis. One new considration that the paper presents is the abilty to filter sounds. The authors also give a way of determining the dispersion of spherical data.
