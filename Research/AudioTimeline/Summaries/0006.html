Author: H. Miinsterberg & A. H. Pierce

Experiment 1

Subjects Tested: 5

Subjects were rotated at a rate of 10 rev. in 10 to 15 sec. and presented with auditory stimulus both during and after rotation. The results for localization during rotation were not decisive. Subjects tended to misplace the sound in the direction opposite of rotation.
