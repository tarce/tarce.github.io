Design Decisions

The author's use of a novel 'auditory pointer' is interesting and can be replicated in VR, however the interaction of sounds is accomplished via mixing and may not be true to life. A possible error in the pointer method used here could occur, as subjects may simply 'match' sounds rather than localize them.
