The Theory

Still others would limit the range of the effectiveness of each of the different factors [time, phase, and intensity] to different frequency ranges, phase being the effective agent in ranges of low frequency, intensity in ranges of high frequency, while time, phase, and intensity, all three, are effective in the intermediate ranges. This, in the writer's opinion, is a more adequate interpretation of the facts; but the range of effectiveness in each case is perhaps too closely defined.
Note:

It is currently accepted that ITD (interaural-time differences) and IPD (interaural-phase differences) contribute at low frequencies and IID (interaurla-intensity differences) and ILD (interaural-level differences) contribute at higher frequencies.
