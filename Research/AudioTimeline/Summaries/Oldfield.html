Design Decisions

In the context of other similar studies, absolute levels of localization performance vary substantially due to differences in the stimulus bandwidths and durations, differences in the amount of variability in spectra between trials, and differences in the methods by which subjects were required to report the apparent locations of sound sources. With a common VR setup, the ability to transfer experiments between researchers for reproducibility would be greatly increased leading to elimination of methodology differences.
