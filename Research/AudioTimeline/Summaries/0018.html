Just Noticeable Differences in Dichotic Phase

Author: J. Zwislocki & R. S. Feldman
Date: September, 1956

  • Timeline
  • Paper

Experiment 1

Subjects Tested: 3
...stimuli consisted of pulses of pure tones which were turned on and off with a rise and fall time of approximately 50 msec...Each pulse was about 1 sec long with an equally long time interval between the two pulses in a pair. The time interval between each two pairs was approximately 3 sec...The listener was required to report whether the second pulse in the pair appeared to the right or left of the first one.
Results
...Around 1300 cps the jnd becomes so great that it cannot be measured. The dichotic time difference calculated from the measured jnd in phase has a minimum near 800 cps.
