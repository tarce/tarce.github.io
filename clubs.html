Clubs

There are many reasons to get involved.
Here are just a few to start you off.

  • DSI

    The Data Science and Informatics Student Organization conducts technical workshops, speaker sessions, discussion groups, and more.

  • Hackathoners

    Hackathons are weekend-long coding competitions. Try one out - its a ton of fun!

  • UF ACM

    UF's student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery, dedicated to computer science research, education, and involvement in CISE.

  • Info SEC

    The Student InfoSec Team assists students in learning information security topics such as penetration testing, reverse engineering, and more.

  • UF WiCSE

    WiCSE is the official student ACM-W chapter at UF, dedicated to increasing the representation of women in Computer Science.

  • UF SEC

    The Software Engineering Club exposes beginner Computer Science students to practical software development technologies and concepts.
